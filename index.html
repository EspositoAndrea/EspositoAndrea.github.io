Curriculum

Andrea Esposito has a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science and Digital Communication at the University of Bari “Aldo Moro”. He is a scholarship researcher at the University of Bari, working on the ISO 25000-certified project “eGLU-Box PA” and as well as in other research activities.

His main interests are in Human-Computer Interaction and Artificial Intelligence. His interests include eXplainable Artificial Intelligence and Human-AI Interaction.

Experiences

  • Early Stage Researcher (Dec 2020 – Present), University of Bari “A. Moro” (Bari (BA), Italy).

    Employed on the project “eGLU-Box”: an ISO 25000-certified system to aid the evaluation of websites’ usability following the eGLU protocol (a usability testing framework and standard). The major duties regarded:

    • the design, implementation, and documentation of a “reverse proxy” to be used in eGLU-Box using Laravel (PHP);
    • the evaluation (and refactoring) of various components of the system.

    During this period, multiple research activities (that are leading to various publications) were started.
